01 Overview

PromptVault is a Chrome Extension that helps you save, organize, search, and inject reusable prompts directly inside ChatGPT. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data — or more accurately, why we don't handle it at all.

We built PromptVault with a simple principle: your prompts are yours. We have no interest in your data, no infrastructure to store it, and no business model that depends on it.

02 Data We Do NOT Collect

The following types of data are never collected, accessed, or transmitted by PromptVault:

✕ Personal information
✕ Email or name
✕ Location data
✕ Browsing history
✕ Keystrokes or clicks
✕ ChatGPT conversations
✕ Financial information
✕ Authentication data
✕ Device identifiers
✕ IP address
✕ Usage analytics
✕ Crash reports
03 What IS Stored (Locally on Your Device)

PromptVault stores the following data exclusively on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser.

✓
Prompt library — titles, text content, tags, star status, and use count for each saved prompt
✓
App settings — your theme preference (dark/light), sidebar position, font size, sort order, and sidebar width

This data is stored solely to make the extension work for you. It is never read by us, never synced to any server, and never shared with anyone.

04 Permissions & Why We Need Them

PromptVault requests the minimum permissions required to function. Here is exactly what each permission does:

storage Saves your prompt library and settings locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. No data is sent anywhere.
activeTab Detects whether you are currently on chatgpt.com before the extension activates. Ensures PromptVault only runs on its intended target page.
scripting Allows the extension icon popup to open the PromptVault sidebar on the active ChatGPT tab when you click "Open PromptVault".
host: chatgpt.com Injects the PromptVault sidebar UI into the ChatGPT interface. This is the core feature — your prompt library lives inside ChatGPT. Limited strictly to chatgpt.com and chat.openai.com.
05 Third Parties & Data Sharing

PromptVault does not integrate with any third-party services. We do not use:

Your data is never sold, rented, shared, or transferred to any third party under any circumstances.

06 Remote Code

PromptVault does not use remote code of any kind. All JavaScript is bundled within the extension package itself. No external scripts, modules, or eval() calls are used.

07 Children's Privacy

PromptVault is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, we collect no data from children either.
